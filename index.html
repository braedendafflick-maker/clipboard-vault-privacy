Privacy Policy for Clipboard Vault

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Clipboard Vault ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Clipboard Vault stores your copied clipboard content (text, links) locally in your browser using the storage permission. This data is used solely to provide clipboard history, search, pinning, and tagging features.

Data Storage

All clipboard data is stored locally on your device. The Extension does not transmit, sell, or share your clipboard content with any third party or external server.

Data Usage

The clipboard data collected is used exclusively to power the core functionality of the Extension (history, search, pin, tag, reuse). It is not used for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Permissions

The Extension requests clipboardRead, clipboardWrite, storage, activeTab, offscreen, and host permissions solely to detect copy events and manage your clipboard history across websites you visit.

Data Retention

You can clear your clipboard history at any time through the Extension's interface. Uninstalling the Extension removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
